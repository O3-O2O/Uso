Project Loved: June 2025

Bask under the sunshine and rainbows and enjoy yourself some fresh Loved picks to start up your summer!

In case you're unfamiliar with what you're reading (or living under a rock), Project Loved hosts community voting polls each month to determine which beatmaps get added to the Loved category. All the beatmaps are chosen by the Loved captains of their respective game modes. You can click on the banners below for each pick to vote on the poll for that map, and there is a description underneath each banner written by a Loved captain. There is also a Project Loved forum as well if you want to view the polls directly. The voting stage lasts for 10 days, so don't forget to vote!

More info can be found on the wiki page and website.
